## Virtual Surgical Planning

1. [op=user_choice] In the "Orbit Model" section, choose Orbit Model node.
2. [op=user_choice] In the "Plate model" section, choose the plate model node.
3. [op=user_choice] In the "Orbit landmarks" section, choose the orbit landmarks node.
4. [op=user_choice] In the "Plate landmarks" section, choose the plate landmarks node.
5. [op=extension_op] Click "Initial registration" button.
6. [op=extension_op] Click "Posterior stop and antero-posterior stops alignment" button.
7. [op=extension_op] Tick the "Enable 3D interaction transform handle" checkbox.
8. [op=user_interaction] Manually adjust the position and rotation of the plate.
9. [op=extension_op] Click "Mark intersection" button.
10. [op=extension_op] Click "Create Distance Heatmap" button.
