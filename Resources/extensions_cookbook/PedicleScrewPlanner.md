## Surgical Planning for Cranial Implant Planning

1. [op=user_choice] In the "Spine CT" section, select the CT volume for processing.
2. [op=extension_op] Click the "Next" button in the "1. Load Image Volume" page.
3. [op=user_interaction] Manually adjust the boundaries of the ROI.
4. [op=user_choice] Choose the "1st Instrumented Level:". Choose the "# Sides:". Choose the "# to Instrument:". Choose the "Approach Direction:".
5. [op=extension_op] Click the "Next" button in the "2. Define Surgical Region of Interest (ROI)" page.
6. [op=extension_op] Click the "Place a control point" button.
7. [op=user_interaction]Manually click in the 2D views to add fiducial points. The total number of points added should be three times the value specified in '# to Instrument'.
8. [op=user_choice] Set the Level/Side/Landmarks following the original selection widget.
9. [op=extension_op] Click the "Next" button in the "3. Place the Landmarks" page.
10. [op=user_choice]Choose the "Choose the puncture site". Choose the "Select screw diametermm".
11. [op=user_interaction] Manually adjust the start and end position of puncture site in 2D views.
12. [op=extension_op] Click the "Update" button.
13. [op=extension_op] Click the "OK" button.
14. [op=branch_op] If every puncture site is configured, jump to step 15. If not, jump to step 10.
15. [op=extension_op] Click the "Next" button in the "4. Adjust Screws" page.
16. [op=extension_op] Click the "Grade Screws" button.
17. [op=review_op] Review the generated Grade Screws output Table following the original results table.
18. [op=extension_op] Click the "Next" button in the "Grading Step" page.
